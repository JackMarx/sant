Coconut Milk

Ingredients:

  • 6 pieces of Sant
  • Coconut milk
  • Sweetener (optional)

Directions:

  1. Infuse Sant in coconut milk for an hour
  2. Add sweetener to taste

Coconut Water

Ingredients:

  • 4-6 pieces of Sant
  • Glass of coconut water

Directions:

  1. Infuse Sant in a glass of coconut water for an hour

Cucumber Lemon Basil Infusion

Ingredients:

  • 5 pieces of Sant
  • Glass of water
  • 4 lemon slices
  • 4 cucumber slices
  • A pinch of chopped basil

Directions:

  1. Infuse Sant, lemon slices, cucumber and basil in a glass of water or reusable water bottle for an hour

Ginger Soda

Ingredients:

  • 6 pieces of Sant
  • Glass of sparkling water
  • 1/2 inch of ginger root
  • Slice of lemon
  • Sweetener (optional)

Directions:

  1. Infuse 6 pieces of Sant in sparkling water for 30 minutes
  2. Add 1/2 inch of giner and squeeze a few drops of lemon juice
  3. Add sweetener to taste

Grapefruit Juice and Rosemary

Ingredients:

  • 5 pieces of Sant
  • Tea strainer
  • Glass of wine
  • Grapefruit juice
  • Fresh rosemary sprig

Directions:

  1. Add rosemary leaves to tea strainer
  2. Infuse rosemary and Sant in a glass of water overnight
  3. Pour grapefruit juice in a glass. Stop at 3/4 full
  4. Top off with the Sant and rosemary infusion

Green Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 15 pieces of Sant
  • 4 kale leaves
  • 1 banana
  • 1 pear

Directions

  1. Blend ingredients in a blender until it creates a smooth consistency

Green Tea and Mint

Ingredients:

  • 4-6 pieces of Sant
  • 1 bag of green tea, steeped in hot water
  • A pinch of chopped mint (optional)

Directions:

  1. Add pieces of Sant to green tea, let infuse for 30 minutes
  2. Sprinkle chopped mint into the tea to get and extra refreshing flavor

Horchata

Ingredients:

  • 4-6 pieces of Sant
  • Rice milk
  • Sweetener

Directions:

  1. Infuse Sant in rice milk for an hour
  2. Add a dash of cinamon
  3. Sweeten to taste

Italian Soda

Ingredients:

  • 6 pieces of Sant
  • Sparkling water
  • Splash of cream
  • Sweetener (optional>

Directions:

  1. Infuse 6 pieces of Sant in sparkling water for an hour
  2. Add a splash of cream
  3. Sweeten to taste

Santgria

Ingredients:

  • 5 pieces of Sant
  • Glass of water
  • Sweetener
  • Cut watermelon
  • Cut apple
  • Sliced grapes

Directions:

  1. Infuse Sant in water overnight
  2. Ass a shot (or two) of vodka to Sant infusion
  3. Add sweetener to taste
  4. Add watermelon, apple, and grapes.
